It is the year 2021.

Rick Astley has taken over the universe and is singing "Never Gonna Give You Up" endlessly and constantly, without end.

However, Rick Astley promises to stop if someone survives an epically hard boss fight, to the tune of Rick's very favorite song.

Can you save the universe from Rick Astley's endless singing? Or will the rick rolls go on forever?

How to play: Use arrow keys or WASD to move. You can play a normal game with 3 lives, or a hardcore game with 1 life.
